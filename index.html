CSCI 426 - Senior Design Capstone - Fall 2023

Projects

During the Fall 2023 semester, teams of students are paired with client mentors to work on the design, development and deployment of a real-world piece of software. This year, the projects include an expansion of a GIS based mobile device to encourage interactive theater engagements, a company store used to motivate and reward community volunteerism, and a Montata state tourism site that provides amenity information for rural areas.
